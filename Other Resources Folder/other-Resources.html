Grants

What is a grant? A grant is a way the government funds your ideas and projects to provide public services and stimulate the economy.A grant is one of many different forms of federal financial assistance. Grants support critical recovery initiatives, innovative research, and many other programs listed in the Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA).
